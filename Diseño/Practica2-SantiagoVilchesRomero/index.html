ABOUT ME

Alvama Ice is a music DJ and producer, known for his mix of genres ranging from reggaeton to hip-hop. His passion for music started at a young age, and he has managed to stand out on the international scene with his fresh and energising style.

Born in Algemesí (Valencia), Alvama Ice started playing in small parties and clubs until his talent led him to perform at renowned festivals. His influences include artists such as David Hood and Jayxme, and his creativity has positioned him as one of the most innovative DJs of the moment.

LAST PRODUCTION

ORIGINS

The inspiration behind the music

"Q No Q No" started as a playful experiment in the studio, inspired by the vibrant nightlife of Algemesí and the energy of Latin rhythms. I wanted to create a track that would capture the essence of spontaneity and fun, blending reggaeton beats with a modern twist. The idea came to me during a festival set where the crowd's reaction sparked the hook that became the heart of this track."

"Producing 'Q No Q No' was an intense but exciting journey. I started with a reggaeton rhythm as the foundation, layering it with dynamic basslines and catchy vocal samples. Collaborating with vocalists from my hometown brought an authentic vibe to the track, while the final touches were crafted in the studio to ensure the song would resonate on the dancefloor and beyond."
