Nature   by   Issey Miyaki

This bathroom furniture piece, designed by Issey Miyake, draws its inspiration from the fluid beauty of nature. The organic lines mimic the soft curves of flowing water, with surfaces that seem to ripple gently like a stream. The materials blend natural wood with delicate stone, evoking the serenity of a forest or a riverbank.

Subtle textures on the cabinet doors resemble leaves or bark, adding a tactile element that invites touch. The design embodies a harmonious balance between simplicity and elegance, offering a peaceful, nature-infused atmosphere to any bathroom space.

The furniture's color palette reflects earthy tones, with soft greens, deep browns, and gentle grays, reminiscent of a tranquil woodland setting. Miyake’s attention to detail is evident in the way light interacts with the surfaces, creating subtle shadows that mimic sunlight filtering through trees.

Flat ID-8 designs and manufactures wooden furniture using timber sourced exclusively from sustainable forests. They prioritize eco-friendly practices, ensuring that each piece is crafted with minimal environmental impact.

Their wood suppliers adhere to strict reforestation guidelines, helping to preserve natural ecosystems while reducing deforestation. Flat ID-8 also focuses on long-lasting craftsmanship, producing high-quality furniture that reduces waste over time. Their commitment to sustainability reflects in both their materials and designs, promoting a greener future for the furniture industry.
