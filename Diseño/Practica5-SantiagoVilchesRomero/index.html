ABOUT ICELAND

Discover the land of fire and ice, where towering glaciers meet steaming geysers, and majestic waterfalls cascade into pristine rivers. Immerse yourself in the untamed beauty of Iceland, a destination like no other, where raw nature takes center stage. From the shimmering Northern Lights to the serene Blue Lagoon, every moment feels like stepping into a dream. Explore volcanic landscapes, lava fields , and rugged coastlines that tell tales of an ancient, dynamic Earth. Experience the unique Viking heritage, intertwined with a vibrant modern culture that celebrates its roots. Adventure, serenity, and wonder await—let Iceland take your breath away!

ICELAND HISTORY

Iceland, a land of dramatic landscapes and deep history. The first settlers, Norse Vikings, arrived in the late 9th century, bringing their rich culture and establishing the first settlements. Over the centuries, Iceland endured harsh climates and isolation, yet developed a strong tradition of saga writing and storytelling. In the 13th century, Iceland became part of the Norwegian Crown, later transitioning to Danish rule. In 1944, it achieved full independence, becoming a republic. The country has since embraced its geothermal energy and unique heritage, balancing modern advancements with the preservation of its natural wonders. Today, Iceland is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, resilient culture, and commitment to sustainability, embodying a history of endurance and innovation.

ICELAND LEGENDS

MĀORI CULTURE

Māori culture is an integral part of life in Aotearoa, New Zealand

Discover New Zealand's fascinating Maori history, full of intrigue and resilience. More than 1,000 years ago, the Maori arrived here in canoes from the Pacific Islands, establishing a unique culture. With the arrival of Europeans in the 17th century, conflicts and tensions over control of the land erupted, culminating in the Land War in the 19th century. However, in the late 20th century, Maori began a cultural renaissance, fighting for their rights and revitalising their language and traditions. New Zealand's Maori history is a testament to the resilience and determination of a people who have left an indelible mark on the country's identity and culture.

FEATURED NATIONAL WILDLIFE

Discover New Zealand's incredible wildlife with three animal highlights. The kiwi, a nocturnal, flightless bird with a long curved beak, is known for its quiet demeanour and ability to sniff out food underground. The kakapo, a nocturnal parrot and the world's heaviest parrot, is flightless and surprisingly flies in its sleep. The tuatara, a prehistoric reptile, is found only in New Zealand and can live more than 100 years. With its unique appearance and a transparent third "skin" over its eyes, the tuatara is a true treasure. These iconic animals represent the diversity and beauty of New Zealand's wildlife.

FEATURED NATIONAL FLORA

Discover New Zealand's impressive flora through three outstanding specimens. The kauri, a monumental tree endemic to the north of the country, can reach heights of over 50 metres and live for over 1,000 years. Flax, known as harakeke in Maori, is a versatile plant that has been used for centuries by Maori to make baskets, clothing and rope. The pohutukawa, also known as the New Zealand Christmas tree, is noted for its vibrant red flowers that bloom during the summer. This tree is a symbol of New Zealand's culture and natural beauty. These three specimens represent the diversity and beauty of New Zealand's flora.

NEW ZEALAND SPECIALITY FOOD

Bluff Oysters are considered a delicacy and are known for their unique taste and texture. Hailing from the Bluff region in the far south of New Zealand, these oysters are farmed in cold, clean water, which gives them their distinctive flavour. Their flesh is tender and juicy, with a mild, salty flavour complemented by a slight sweetness. Bluff Oysters are prized both nationally and internationally, and their harvest season, which runs from March to August, is eagerly anticipated. Whether served fresh with a squeeze of lemon or prepared in delicious recipes, Bluff Oysters are a true seafood lover's delight - discover the unique flavour and deliciousness of New Zealand Bluff Oysters!
