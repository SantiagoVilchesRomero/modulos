Our Team

Majesty

The kitchen at Disfrutar restaurant is led by chefs Oriol Castro, Mateu Casañas, and Eduard Xatruch.

"As chefs, we have honed a taste proficiency that heightens the senses with a pure balance. Collaborating with our team, we tirelessly strive to provide our guests with a holistic gastronomic experience at all times."

"Every day, we pursue perfection in the culinary ensemble, encompassing everything from signage to location, atmosphere, and the overall environment. In essence, we aspire for our guests to leave the restaurant with cherished memories that will endure."

"The entire Disfrutar team shares a deep passion for their craft. We consistently seek perfection in every detail, as only through this can we aspire to deliver a unique and complete gastronomic experience."

Singular experience

At Disfrutar, our chefs are passionately dedicated to ensuring that each guest experiences a unique gastronomic journey in our restaurant.

"We strive to captivate your senses, offering not just an exceptional meal, but an unforgettable culinary adventure."

"In every creation, our chefs aim to surpass the ordinary, creating moments that will linger in your memory, transforming your visit into more than just a meal: an experience resonating with the authenticity and excellence that defines us."

"Furthermore, Disfrutar is not simply a restaurant; it's a journey through the art of gastronomy. From the welcoming ambiance that embraces you to the meticulously curated wine selection that enhances our dishes, every detail is crafted to immerse you in a world where passion meets palate."
