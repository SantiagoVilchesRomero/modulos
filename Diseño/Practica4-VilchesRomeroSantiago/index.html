Welcome to Disfrutar

Our History

The founder of the restaurant, Oriol Castro.

"Born from a profound passion for gastronomy and a desire to create an unparalleled culinary experience, Disfrutar (meaning “Enjoy” in English) opened its doors in December 2014. Our restaurant is the culmination of years of dedicated work at elBulli, where Mateu Casañas, Oriol Castro, and Eduard Xatruch honed their skills and formed their professional bond, further solidifying their culinary excellence at Compartir, which opened in April 2012 in Cadaqués."
"We take immense pride in sourcing the finest local ingredients to craft dishes that not only delight the senses but also tell a story of culinary artistry. Our committed team of chefs, each a maestro in their own field, brings innovation to traditional recipes, ensuring a dining experience that is both nostalgic and avant-garde."
"Disfrutar is more than just a restaurant; it is a journey through the art of gastronomy. From the welcoming ambiance that envelops you to the meticulously curated wine list that enhances our dishes, every detail is designed to immerse you in a world where passion meets the palate."
